# Problem Statement

Managing personal finances has become increasingly challenging due to multiple income sources, digital payment methods, recurring subscriptions, and growing financial responsibilities. Existing finance applications primarily focus on recording transactions and generating reports, but they often lack intelligent analysis, predictive insights, and personalized financial guidance.

IntelliWealth aims to bridge this gap by combining personal finance management with Artificial Intelligence. The platform enables users to track income and expenses, manage budgets, analyze spending habits, forecast future financial trends, detect unusual transactions, and receive AI-driven recommendations for better financial decision-making.

The system is initially designed for Indian users, supporting Indian financial practices such as UPI payments, GST-based receipts, monthly salary cycles, and educational investment guidance.
